About Me

I am a Machine Learning Scientist with extensive experience working with Large Language Models (LLMs) and deploying AI solutions in production environments. Over the past decade, I have been at the forefront of both academic and industrial research in AI, particularly focusing on Conversational AI, NLP, and Generative AI.


My passion lies in bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and practical implementation. I excel at bringing innovative concepts into production, ensuring that research doesn't just stay theoretical but results in impactful, real-world applications. I have successfully led numerous research projects, seeing them through from ideation to deployment, often guiding cross-functional teams to turn complex machine learning models into scalable products.


With a strong foundation in Neural Network architectures—including RNNs, LSTMs, Transformers, and CNNs—I have built and optimized AI systems that power intelligent conversational experiences. My focus is on ensuring that each AI model I develop not only achieves state-of-the-art performance but also integrates seamlessly into production, providing tangible value to users and stakeholders.
